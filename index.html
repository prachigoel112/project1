About Us!

Welcome to Shiva Polymers, a leading innovator in plastic recycling and sustainable packaging solutions. We specialize in producing high-quality Linear Low-Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) and packaging materials with a thickness of 120 microns, catering to a wide range of industries and applications. Our commitment to environmental stewardship drives us to continuously improve our recycling processes, ensuring that we minimize waste and maximize the reuse of valuable resources. At Shiva Polymers, we are dedicated to creating a greener, more sustainable future through our innovative products and eco-friendly practices.

Linear Low-Density Polyethylene

LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) is a versatile and highly flexible plastic polymer known for its strength and durability. Our LLDPE products are manufactured using advanced technology, ensuring consistent quality and performance. With excellent puncture resistance and a high tensile strength, LLDPE is ideal for a wide range of applications, including packaging, agriculture, and construction. Its low cost, lightweight nature, and superior barrier properties make it an excellent choice for protecting and preserving a variety of products. Whether you need to package food, chemicals, or other goods, our LLDPE products are designed to meet your needs and exceed your expectations.
